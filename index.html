Risk Management

Critical Path & Risks

Critical

SoluSoft ERP API Availability

#1 risk factor — custom connector (470 hrs) depends entirely on API readiness. Must confirm in Discovery Week 1.

Critical

Pre-Book Workflow Adoption

Replaces retailers' Excel-based seasonal ordering. UX must be validated early with actual retailers to ensure adoption.

High

Budget & Timeline Pressure

$318K / 1,880 hours / 18-22 weeks is ambitious. SOW notes client delays can mean "several weeks delay." Content delivery is critical path.

High

Bilingual Content Management

All catalog content must be maintained in EN/FR. Brand assets come from manufacturers — delivery timing is a dependency.

Medium

Integration Hours Burn Rate

470 hours for custom ERP connector is substantial and could expand if SoluSoft API has undocumented limitations.

Strategy

Launch Recommendations

1

Confirm SoluSoft API Immediately

Single biggest risk factor. Get API documentation, access credentials, and sandbox availability confirmed in Discovery Week 1.

2

Validate Pre-Book UX with Retailers

Replace Excel-based seasonal ordering habits. Test with 2-3 key retailers early — their feedback shapes the B2B portal design.

3

Phased Go-Live: D2C First, B2B Second

De-risk by launching the D2C storefront first. If integration testing reveals issues, B2B follows independently.

4

Monitor Integration Hours Burn Rate

470 hours for custom connector could expand. Set weekly check-ins on integration progress starting Week 4.

5

Marketing Function Review

Mario wants to reassess team structure. Lean on Molsoft + Shopify AI (Sidekick, content tools) to guide decisions post-launch.

Sidekick
How do I A/B test a new homepage hero banner without affecting our live site?
Great question! You can use Rollouts — Shopify's native A/B testing tool. Here's how:

1. Go to Online Store → Themes
2. Click "Create rollout"
3. Set a traffic split (e.g. 25% see the new version)
4. Edit the hero banner in the theme editor — changes only apply to the rollout
Can I schedule when it goes live?
Yes! You can schedule rollouts to start and end at specific dates and times. Your live store stays completely untouched until you choose to publish. You can also run multiple rollouts simultaneously.
What else can we test with Rollouts?
You can test any theme-level change: layouts, sections, content blocks, CTAs, product page structure, collection pages, and navigation. Shopify handles the traffic splitting automatically and keeps visitors consistent during their session.
